Case Study · AulaCube

Modernizing school management for India — designing a dual-sided platform from 0 to 1

DurationJul 2023 — May 2024
PlatformWeb · Mobile
My RoleLead Product Designer
Focus0–1 Product · UI/UX Design
Overview

As the sole designer at an early-stage startup, I led the end-to-end design of a dual-sided platform: a robust Student Management System (SMS) for schools and a B2C Parent App — both built from scratch.

Our mission was to bridge the communication gap between educational institutions and homes. By replacing fragmented, legacy software with a unified digital ecosystem, we aimed to create a world where parents are active participants in their child's journey rather than passive observers.

"We didn't just need to build a better database — we needed to build a communication bridge that felt as intuitive as the social apps people use every day."

Problem Space

Three critical pain points in Indian school management

The Indian EdTech market for school management was saturated but stagnant. Through competitive audits and discovery, we identified three core issues.

01 · Legacy Debt

Outdated interfaces

Existing products featured cluttered, Windows 95-era interfaces that led to low adoption rates among time-poor teachers.

02 · Fragmentation

Disconnected tools

Schools juggled separate tools for attendance, fee collection, and parent communication — data lived in silos with no integration.

03 · Information Gap

Parent blackout

Parents had little visibility into their child's school life beyond quarterly parent-teacher meetings or emergency calls.

Solution

Two distinct but interconnected products, one source of truth

I designed two interfaces that shared a single data backbone — so actions in one (like marking attendance) automatically updated the other (monthly reports, parent notifications).

Aula Edge — School Management System (B2B)

An all-in-one dashboard replacing the "Frankenstein" suite of tools schools relied on. I integrated attendance, grading, fee management, scheduling, admissions, role management, and an AI-powered Analytics Copilot into a single cohesive UI — prioritising cleanliness and clarity over data density.

01Unified dashboard with real-time student, staff, and fee overviews
02Multi-modal attendance marking with class-wise and staff views
03Fee management with collection tracking, overdue alerts, and payment modes
04Report card builder with scholastic and co-scholastic grading
05Admissions pipeline with lead tracking and CRM-style Kanban flow
06Analytics Copilot — AI assistant for identifying at-risk students

Aula Connect — Parent App (B2C)

A mobile-first app designed to feel as familiar as a social app while delivering meaningful school data. Parents could track attendance, view announcements, monitor fee status, and stay connected to their child's daily school life in one place.
